## Double Hazy Jane #1

**Street Name**: TBD
**Style**: Double NEIPA
**Recipe**: `1443929.json` (community)
**Brewed**: 2026-01-04
**Status**: Fermenting (Secondary)

### Adjustments from Base Recipe
- Reduced Wheat Malt: 260g → 120g
- Reduced Rice Hulls: 100g → 80g
- Increased Pale Malt: 1000g → 1130g
- Substituted Extra Pale Malt for Pale Malt (using expiring stock)

### Pitch

A bold double NEIPA bursting with tropical hop character. Massively dry-hopped with Amarillo, Citra, Mosaic, and Simcoe for intense notes of mango, citrus, and stone fruit. Smooth mouthfeel from flaked oats and wheat malt, with a hazy golden pour. At 9% ABV, it packs a punch while remaining dangerously drinkable.

### Stats
- **ABV**: 9.04%
- **IBU**: 13
- **OG**: 1.081
- **FG**: 1.016 (estimated)

### Key Ingredients
- Pale Malt (1130g), Maris Otter (1010g)
- Wheat Malt (120g), Flaked Oats (100g)
- Hops: Chinook, Simcoe, Amarillo (boil)
- Dry Hops: Amarillo, Citra, Mosaic, Simcoe (64g total)

## Colvestone

**Street Name**: Colvestone
**Style**: Berry Mead (Melomel)
**Recipe**: `berry-mead.json` (custom)
**Brewed**: 2025-01-03
**Status**: Bottled (2025-09-09)

### Adjustments from Base Recipe
- Added wood chips, citrus, and spices during secondary for complexity

### Pitch

A vibrant melomel bursting with the rich, fruity character of mixed berries complemented by golden honey. At nearly 10% ABV, this fruit-forward mead balances sweetness with berry tartness for a refreshing, complex drinking experience.

### Stats
- **ABV**: 9.98%
- **IBU**: 0
- **OG**: 1.090
- **FG**: 1.014

### Key Ingredients
- Honey (1360g)
- Frozen mixed berries (250g)
- Water (3300ml)
- Yeast + nutrients

### Bottling Variants

|-------------|---------|-----------|-------|
