About This Brew

A stunning ruby-hued mead showcasing the vibrant tartness of hibiscus flowers balanced with golden honey sweetness. Warming notes of ginger and cinnamon add depth and complexity, creating a beautifully layered drinking experience. The floral character shines through at 11.8% ABV, making this a sophisticated sipper with eye-catching color.
