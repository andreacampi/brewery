About This Brew

A traditional mead inspired by the legendary Joe's Ancient Orange recipe. Whole citrus fruit—half an orange and three clementines—ferment alongside warming cinnamon, creating a complex interplay of sweet honey, bright citrus zest, and aromatic spice. At 18.67% ABV, this is a sipping mead with incredible depth and character. All bottles have been enjoyed.
