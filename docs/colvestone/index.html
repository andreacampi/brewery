About This Brew

A vibrant melomel bursting with the rich, fruity character of mixed berries complemented by golden honey. Experimentation with oak chips, citrus, and warming spices during maturation creates unique flavor profiles across different bottles. At nearly 10% ABV, this fruit-forward mead balances sweetness with berry tartness for a refreshing, complex drinking experience.
