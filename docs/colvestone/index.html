About This Brew

A rich, warming mead built around deep blackberry fruit and a touch of spice. Whole blackberries are mashed and infused with allspice, cloves, and fresh lemon before fermentation, giving the mead an aromatic backbone that softens beautifully over time. Slightly backsweetened to balance the natural tartness of the fruit, with a dry, wine-like finish.
