About This Brew

A refreshing citrus mead showcasing the natural sweetness of honey brightened with fresh lemon. Partial caramelization of the honey adds depth and complexity, while the citrus provides a crisp, clean finish. Light-bodied and elegantly balanced at 14% ABV, perfect for sipping chilled.
